Regular Tic-Tac-Toe

Game board:
|----> A 5x5 square grid.

Turn Order: 
|----> The system randomly determines which player (X or O) goes first.

Turns: 
|----> Players take turns placing their respective shapes (X or O) on any empty cell of the grid.

Winning Condition: 
|----> The first player to place 3 of their shapes in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
